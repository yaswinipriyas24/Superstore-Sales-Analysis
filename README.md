# Superstore Sales Strategy Dashboard
**An End-to-End BI Project: From Raw Data to Executive Insights**

##  Objective
To identify the "Profit Leaks" and "Growth Engines" within the Superstore ecosystem by answering 10 critical business questions regarding Sales, Profit, and Operations.

## The 9-Question Framework
1. **Geographic Performance:** Where are we losing money? (Map)
2. **Growth Trend:** Is our profit keeping pace with sales growth? (Line Chart)
3. **Category Health:** Which products drive the most margin? (Bar Chart)
4. **Customer Loyalty:** Who are our Top 10 VIPs? (Rank Chart)
5. **Efficiency:** How do discounts impact our bottom line? (Scatter Plot)
6. **Operations:** What is our average shipping lead time? (KPI)
7. **Market Fit:** Which segments (Corporate vs. Consumer) are most profitable? (Treemap)
8. **Inventory Risk:** Which products are our top 10 "Loss Leaders"? (Bottom Rank)
9. **Executive Summary:** Total Sales & Profit snapshots (KPIs)


##  Key Takeaways
- **The Discount Trap:** Analysis proved that discounts over 20% destroy profit margins.
- **Shipping Bottlenecks:** Certain regions average 6+ days to ship, risking customer churn.
- **Segment Focus:** The 'Home Office' segment, while smaller, offers the highest profit ratio.
